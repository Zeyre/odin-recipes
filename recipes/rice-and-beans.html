Rice and Beans

Description

Rice and Beans is a satisfying dish with the focus being on the saucy beans. The rice soaks in the juices and pairs wonderfully with your favorite meats.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 2 tbsp. Sofrito
  • 1 packet Sazon
  • 1/2 packet Chicken Flavored Bouillon
  • 1/8 tsp. Oregano Leaf
  • 1/2 cup Tomato Sauce
  • 1 can Pink Beans
  • Adobo to taste
  • 2 cups Long Grain rice
