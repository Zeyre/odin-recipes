Pastelillos de Carne

Description

Pastelillos de Carne are small turnovers or meat pockets made with thin dough. We crimp the edges to keep the contents inside while we fry it to give it flakiness and crunchiness.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1/4 cup sofrito
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 1/2 tsp. adobo
  • 2 small potatoes
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • pastelillo disc

Steps

  1. Heat skillet at med-high, drizzle 2 tbsp. of olive oil
  2. Saute onions, garlic, and sofrito until onions are translucent
  3. Drizzle beef with tbsp. of olive oil and seaosn with adobo and salt
  4. Add beef, potatoes, bay leaves, water, and tomato paste to skillet
  5. Stir until well combined raise heat to bring to boil
  6. Lower heat to simmer, cover and let cook for 15 minutes
  7. Uncover and let simmer for 15 more minutes
  8. Place 1 spoonful onto pastelillo disc
  9. Fold in half and crimp the edges with a fork. Repeat as needed
  10. Heat 1 1/2 inches of oil to 350 degrees in deep skillet
  11. Cook each side for 2 minutes or until crispy golden brown
  12. Move to plate and let drain and cool
